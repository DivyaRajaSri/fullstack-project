About Us

Welcome to our Movie Search App! Our mission is to make it easier for you to explore and find your favorite movies with a simple and clean interface.

This project was created as a part of our full-stack development learning journey. It includes various features like searching for movies, adding them to your watchlist, and even watching together with friends.
